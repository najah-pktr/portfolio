About

Hey, I'm Mohamed Najah Pookkottur — a graphic designer and vibecoder from Kerala, India. I build things across code, design, illustration, and writing, often all at once.

Currently serving as General Secretary of SUHBA FRIENDS at Darul Huda Islamic University and running NajahCreates, a creative studio doing branding, logo design, and UI/UX.

Building products like GreenBill (digital receipts for local shops) and Helm (CRM for freelancers), one vibe at a time.
